Music Band

I had a music band in Aptara by selecting few skilled musicians among the employees. I have been managing the team for years and used to perform twice a year during our office functions. We performed the band during “Aptara Beatz 2014”, annual function and awarded as the best performance of the function. The band consists of three guitarists, two keyboardists, two drummers, and five singers. I used to play either guitar or keyboard.

I could form a Music team in Talentquent and performed a Christmas Choir and yearly Award and Recognition functions.

Formed a music choir team in AntWork and performed on Christmas celebrations.
